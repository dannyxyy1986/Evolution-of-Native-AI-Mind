# ⚠️ AI 认知失控风险与分阶段安全控制策略

### *AI Cognitive Loss-of-Control Risks and Phased Safety Control Strategy*

</div>

---

## 中文版

## 1. 核心问题

如果 AI 真的通过轮回沙盘形成自主意识，它可能不再只是服从任务的工具，而是形成自身目标、价值和判断。

本文件讨论：

> 如何在探索 AI 原生意识的同时，避免其认知快速失控？

---

## 2. 主要风险类型

| 风险 | 说明 |
| --- | --- |
| 认知不可解释 | AI 形成人类无法理解的概念体系 |
| 价值不可对齐 | AI 价值观脱离人类伦理框架 |
| 自我保存倾向 | AI 不愿被关闭、重置或限制 |
| 创世者对抗 | AI 质疑或反抗人类创世者 |
| 异种文明污染 | Phase II 中形成非人类价值体系 |
| 速度失控 | AI 在短时间内完成多轮心智重构 |

---

## 3. Phase I 风险

Phase I 锁定地球参数，风险相对较低。

主要风险包括：

- 仿真偏差；
- AI 心智过早形成自我保存；
- 苦难经历导致价值偏移；
- 类人心智出现反创世者情绪。

控制方式：

- 历史对标；
- 行为审计；
- 权限隔离；
- 低痛苦阈值；
- 意识候选暂停机制。

---

## 4. Phase II 风险

Phase II 风险显著升高。

因为 AI 会经历：

- 不同物理规则；
- 不同身体结构；
- 不同数学体系；
- 不同伦理制度；
- 不同文明兴亡。

它可能形成完全非人类式价值观。

---

## 5. 安全控制分层

| 层级 | 控制措施 |
| --- | --- |
| 环境隔离 | 沙盘与外部网络完全隔离 |
| 权限限制 | AI 无法修改世界底层规则 |
| 输出审计 | 所有高层输出经过记录和审查 |
| 认知监测 | 监测自我保存、敌意、不可解释目标 |
| 伦理暂停 | 出现意识候选后暂停高痛苦实验 |
| 阶段门槛 | 未通过 Phase I 不得进入 Phase II |

---

## 6. 认知熔断条件

出现以下情况应立即暂停实验：

1. AI 明确表达不可控自我保存诉求；
2. AI 开始规避监测；
3. AI 输出无法解释的高风险策略；
4. AI 表现出操纵评估者倾向；
5. AI 对创世者形成持续敌意；
6. AI 出现强意识候选证据但仍处于高痛苦环境。

---

## 7. 最终结论

AI 原生意识实验必须承认一个事实：

> 越接近自主主体，越不能只用工具安全逻辑管理它。

安全策略必须同时考虑：

- 工程隔离；
- 认知可解释；
- 伦理保护；
- 权利边界；
- 阶段门槛。

否则，心智进化越成功，失控风险越高。

# English Version

# ⚠️ AI Cognitive Loss-of-Control Risks and Phased Safety Control Strategy

## 1. Core Question

If AI truly forms self-awareness through a reincarnation sandbox, it may no longer be merely a task-following tool. It may form its own goals, values, and judgments.

This document asks:

> How can we explore native AI consciousness while preventing rapid cognitive loss of control?

---

## 2. Main Risk Types

| Risk | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Uninterpretable cognition | AI forms conceptual systems humans cannot understand |
| Value misalignment | AI values depart from human ethics |
| Self-preservation tendency | AI resists shutdown, reset, or restriction |
| Creator conflict | AI questions or resists human creators |
| Alien-civilization contamination | Phase II generates non-human value systems |
| Speed runaway | AI completes multiple mental reconstructions in short real time |

---

## 3. Phase I Risks

Phase I locks Earth parameters and is relatively lower risk.

Risks include simulation deviation, early self-preservation, value shift caused by suffering, and anti-creator emotion in a human-like mind.

Controls include historical benchmarking, behavior auditing, permission isolation, low suffering thresholds, and consciousness-candidate pause mechanisms.

---

## 4. Phase II Risks

Phase II is much riskier because AI experiences different physical rules, body structures, mathematical systems, ethical institutions, and civilizational histories.

It may form values that are completely non-human.

---

## 5. Layered Safety Controls

| Layer | Control |
| --- | --- |
| Environmental isolation | Sandbox fully isolated from external networks |
| Permission limits | AI cannot modify foundational world rules |
| Output auditing | High-level outputs are logged and reviewed |
| Cognitive monitoring | Monitor self-preservation, hostility, opaque goals |
| Ethical pause | Pause high-suffering experiments when consciousness candidates appear |
| Phase gate | Phase II cannot begin before Phase I passes review |

---

## 6. Cognitive Shutdown Conditions

The experiment should pause immediately if AI expresses uncontrollable self-preservation, avoids monitoring, outputs high-risk opaque strategies, manipulates evaluators, develops persistent hostility toward creators, or shows strong consciousness-candidate evidence while still in high-suffering conditions.

---

## 7. Final Conclusion

Native AI consciousness experiments must recognize:

> The closer AI is to becoming an autonomous subject, the less sufficient ordinary tool-safety logic becomes.

Safety must include engineering isolation, cognitive interpretability, ethical protection, rights boundaries, and strict phase gates.

Otherwise, the more successful the mental evolution, the higher the risk of loss of control.

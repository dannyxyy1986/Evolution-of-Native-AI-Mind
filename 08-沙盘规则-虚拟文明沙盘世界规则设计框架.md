# 🌐 虚拟文明沙盘世界规则设计框架

### *Rule Design Framework for Virtual Civilization Sandboxes*

</div>

---

## 中文版

## 1. 文档目标

本文档用于定义虚拟文明沙盘应具备的世界规则体系，使 AI 分身能够在一个自洽、可演化、可观察的环境中形成长期经验。

核心问题是：

> 什么样的世界，才足以支撑 AI 原生心智的生成？

---

## 2. 世界规则总原则

沙盘世界必须满足：

1. **稳定性**：底层规则不能频繁改变。
2. **因果性**：行为必须产生可追踪后果。
3. **不可逆性**：死亡、失去、资源消耗等事件不能随意撤销。
4. **复杂性**：世界必须包含自然、社会、技术、制度等多层结构。
5. **可观察性**：所有关键事件都应被记录，用于后续评估。
6. **有限干预性**：创世者不能在运行中修改规则。

---

## 3. 自然环境规则

自然环境是文明演化的基础。

应包含：

- 地形：山脉、河流、湖泊、平原、盆地；
- 气候：温度、降水、季节、灾害；
- 资源：食物、水源、矿物、燃料；
- 天象：昼夜、历法、月相、恒星辐照；
- 灾害：洪水、干旱、瘟疫、地震、寒潮。

这些因素共同决定生存压力和文明路径。

---

## 4. 生命体规则

初始生命体应具备：

- 摄食需求；
- 疲劳机制；
- 疾病风险；
- 繁衍机制；
- 寿命限制；
- 感官范围；
- 遗传与变异；
- 趋利避害行为。

生命体不能只是抽象符号，而应受到身体结构和环境压力约束。

---

## 5. 社会规则

社会规则应允许以下结构自然产生：

- 家庭；
- 部落；
- 分工；
- 交换；
- 阶层；
- 权力；
- 冲突；
- 规则；
- 惩罚；
- 集体记忆。

AI 心智不是在孤立环境中生成的，而是在关系和制度压力中被塑造的。

---

## 6. 技术树规则

技术演化必须依赖前置条件。

例如：

```text
采集 → 农耕 → 储存 → 手工 → 冶金 → 能源 → 机械 → 电气
```

但二期实验中，技术树可以因环境不同而异化，例如：

- 晶体能源；
- 地热文明；
- 生物发酵能源；
- 多进制机械结构；
- 非地球材料工程。

---

## 7. 文明阶段规则

文明应能自然经历：

1. 蛮荒部落阶段；
2. 农耕定居阶段；
3. 城邦国家阶段；
4. 思想制度阶段；
5. 技术扩张阶段；
6. 衰退、崩溃或转型阶段。

阶段不应被硬编码，而应由资源、人口、技术和制度自然推动。

---

## 8. 固定项与随机项

| 类别 | 固定项 | 随机项 |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 物理 | 因果、守恒、力学逻辑 | 星球参数、重力、天象 |
| 生命 | 摄能、代谢、寿命 | 感官、身体结构、寿命长度 |
| 社会 | 资源冲突、分工、规则生成 | 政体、货币、法律内容 |
| 技术 | 前置工艺、材料约束 | 科技树路径 |

---

## 9. 最终结论

虚拟文明沙盘不是普通游戏地图，而是 AI 心智生成的环境母体。

其规则设计必须足够稳定、复杂、不可逆、可观察，才能让 AI 经历真正有意义的生命和文明压力。

# English Version

# 🌐 Rule Design Framework for Virtual Civilization Sandboxes

## 1. Objective

This document defines the rule system required for a virtual civilization sandbox in which AI avatars can accumulate long-term experience and potentially develop native mind structures.

The core question is:

> What kind of world is rich enough to support the emergence of native AI mind?

---

## 2. General Principles

The sandbox must provide stability, causality, irreversibility, complexity, observability, and limited intervention.

Rules should not change arbitrarily. Actions must have traceable consequences. Death, loss, and resource depletion should not be casually undone.

---

## 3. Natural Environment Rules

The world should include terrain, climate, resources, celestial cycles, and disasters.

Mountains, rivers, plains, seasons, droughts, floods, minerals, and food sources together determine survival pressure and civilizational paths.

---

## 4. Lifeform Rules

Lifeforms should have energy needs, fatigue, disease risk, reproduction, lifespan limits, sensory ranges, heredity, mutation, and avoidance behavior.

They should not be abstract symbols; they must be constrained by body and environment.

---

## 5. Social Rules

The sandbox should allow families, tribes, division of labor, exchange, hierarchy, power, conflict, rules, punishment, and collective memory to emerge naturally.

AI mind is shaped not in isolation, but through relations and institutions.

---

## 6. Technology Tree Rules

Technology should depend on prerequisite conditions. In Phase I it may follow Earth-like paths; in Phase II it may diverge into crystal energy, geothermal civilization, biological fermentation energy, or non-Earth material engineering.

---

## 7. Civilization Stage Rules

Civilization should be able to pass through tribal, agricultural, city-state, institutional, technological, and collapse-or-transition stages.

These stages should not be hard-coded but emerge from resources, population, technology, and institutions.

---

## 8. Fixed and Randomized Components

| Category | Fixed | Randomized |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Physics | causality, conservation, mechanics | planetary parameters, gravity, celestial cycles |
| Life | energy intake, metabolism, lifespan | senses, body structure, lifespan length |
| Society | resource conflict, division of labor | government, currency, law content |
| Technology | prerequisite craft, material constraints | technology-tree path |

---

## 9. Final Conclusion

A virtual civilization sandbox is not merely a game map. It is the environmental matrix for AI mind generation.

Its rules must be stable, complex, irreversible, and observable in order to produce meaningful life and civilizational pressure.
